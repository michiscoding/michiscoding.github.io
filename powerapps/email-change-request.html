email change request

pages, flows — april 9 2026

what it does

this power page allows signed in users to navigate to a page on their ecomerce site to update their email. the page autopopulates the user's current email address, and intakes the new email address to update their current email address with. upon submission of the form, users receive an email at their new email address with a link to verify their identity. upon verification, the email is updated in d365 and ecommerce.

how it works

this solution uses power pages, power automate, custom connectors, and dataverse to complete the automation.
